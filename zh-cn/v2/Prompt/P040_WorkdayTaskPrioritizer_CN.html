🚀 直接复制这段 Prompt（简中版）
嘿 Copilot，帮我整顿今天的工作节奏！先分析我的邮件、日历邀请和 Teams 对话，将任务依优先级分为三类并各自列表：Must-Do（紧急且重要）、Should-Do（重要但不紧急）、Could-Do（有就更好，可弹性安排）。在表格中包含以下字段：任务名称、指派者、截止时间（如有）、建议运行时段。针对每个优先级，依据我的日历建议最佳时间段，并加入维持专注的小技巧，例如封锁安静时段或将类似任务集中处理。然后加一点趣味：根据我今天的任务产生一句激励标语，例如「征服收件箱大山！」最后，建议一件我可以在一天结束时做的事，放松并庆祝今天的进步。
📋 Prompt Template (English)
Hey Copilot, let's get my workday in shape! Start by analyzing my emails, calendar invites, and Teams chats to prioritize my tasks. Organize them into three categories with tables for each: Must-Do (urgent and important), Should-Do (important but not urgent), and Could-Do (good to have but flexible). In the tables, include columns for Task, Who Assigned It, Deadline (if applicable), and Recommended Time Slot(s). For each priority, suggest the best time slots based on my calendar to tackle them and include tips to stay focused—like blocking quiet time or grouping similar tasks. Then, add a touch of fun: create a motivational tagline for the day based on my tasks, like 'Conquer the Inbox Mountain!' or 'Presentation Prep, Like a Pro!' Finally, suggest one thing I can do to wind down and celebrate progress at the end of the day.
💡 三个典型使用场景
1
🌅 周一早晨启动仪式

2
🔄 临时插入紧急事项
突发事件出现时再跑一次，即时刷新任务顺序，确保最重要的事不被挤掉。
3
🌙 下班前回顾对照
对照早上的计划，确认今日进度并顺畅交接明日任务，清爽地结束一天。
