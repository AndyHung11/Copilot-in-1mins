✍️ Text Simplifier
✨ Jargon out, plain words in
Spend long enough in a field and "knowing too much" becomes its own communication problem.

This Prompt makes Copilot a rewriting expert—it replays your text in simpler, plainer language while preserving the original meaning, and adds a glossary at the end so readers can look up unfamiliar terms.

Whether it's a technical white paper, internal policy doc or a customer-facing letter, paste the text in and Copilot makes it readable for everyone!
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
Act as a rewriting expert. Your task is to rewrite the following text to make it easier for a general audience to understand. The rewritten text should keep the original meaning but use simpler, more accessible language. The goal is to ensure that everyone can understand the information. Make sure the text does not include jargon or complicated terms. Suggest a glossary below the text. The tone should stay professional but friendly.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
擔任改寫專家。你的任務是將以下文字改寫，使一般受眾更容易理解。改寫後的文字應保留原本意思，但使用更簡單、更易懂的語言。目標是確保所有人都能理解這些資訊。請確保文字不包含行業術語或複雜詞彙。在文字下方建議一份詞彙表。語氣應保持專業但友善。
