✨ Weekly Team Pulse Survey
This prompt creates a lightweight, mobile-friendly weekly pulse survey you can use in any team or function. It keeps questions neutral, uses a 5-point Likert scale plus one open text for nuance, and includes intro/thank-you copy so you can share immediately.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
※ Copy the entire block and paste directly into Copilot Chat (Web)
Create a 3-minute weekly 'Team Pulse' survey suitable for any industry and 10–200 respondents. • Sections: Workload, Progress, Blockers, Support, Open Feedback. • Question types: 4 Likert (5-point, clearly labeled ends), 1 optional free-text. • Wording: neutral, plain language (B2 reading level), one idea per question. • Include: a short, friendly intro that explains purpose and anonymity; a brief thank-you message with next steps (how results will be used). Keep it concise and easy to complete on mobile. Provide the full question set and answer options.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
請建立一份適合任何產業、3 分鐘可完成的每週「團隊脈動」問卷，適用 10-200 名受訪者。• 面向：工作量、進度、阻礙、支援、開放回饋。• 問題類型：4 道 Likert 量表（5 點，兩端有清楚說明）、1 道選填自由作答題。• 措辭：中立、平易（B2 閱讀程度），每題只問一個概念。• 包含：簡短友善的說明（解釋目的與匿名性）；簡短感謝語，說明結果如何被使用。保持簡潔，讓手機也能輕鬆填答。提供完整題目與選項。
