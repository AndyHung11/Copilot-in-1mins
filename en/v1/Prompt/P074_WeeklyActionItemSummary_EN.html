💬 Weekly Action Item Summary
✨ One click. Every commitment. 🚀
Action items get scattered across emails, Teams threads, meeting notes, and chat replies—and by Friday, you can't remember what you actually committed to, or to whom.

This prompt has Copilot scan the week's communications and pull out every action item assigned to you—with the source, the deadline (or implied urgency), and who is waiting on it.

One list. Every commitment. No more "I forgot you asked me that."
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
Please compile all action items assigned to me last week from my emails, meeting transcripts, Teams messages, and file comments where I was @-mentioned. Include any tasks, follow-up requests, or deadlines that others raised for me. Create a table with the following columns:
1) Action Item
2) Who Assigned It
3) Requested Due Date
4) Status (if mentioned)
If no due date was mentioned, leave the field blank.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
請彙整上週透過 電子郵件、會議逐字稿、Teams 訊息、以及我被 @ 提及的檔案註解 中所有指派給我的待辦事項。包含任何別人提到的任務、後續追蹤請求或截止日期。請建立一張表格，包含以下欄位：
1) 待辦事項（Action Item）
2) 由誰指派（Who Assigned It）
3) 請求的截止日（Requested Due Date）
4) 狀態（Status）（若有提到）
若沒有提到截止日，欄位請留空。
💡 Usage Tips
1. ⏰ Schedule it for every Monday — use aka.ms/ScheduledPrompt to run it Mondays at 8:30, so the list lands in your inbox before you start.
2. 📌 Pipe it into Planner / To Do — copy the output table into Microsoft To Do or Planner to turn it into trackable tasks instantly.
3. 🔍 Swap the time range — change "last week" to "the past 14 days," "this month," or "this quarter" to match weekly, biweekly, monthly, or quarterly reviews.
