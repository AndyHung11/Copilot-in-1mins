🗣️ Workplace Language Coach
✨ Your private workplace tutor
The hardest part of learning a new language isn't memorizing words — it's finding chances to actually speak, and knowing what you got wrong when you do.

This prompt turns Copilot into your personal language coach. It asks which language and what level you're at, then designs conversation drills around your job role and real work scenarios. After every answer you get instant grammar feedback and better phrasing so you build real workplace fluency.

Commute, lunch break, waiting for a call — practice anytime, and feel the progress!
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
※ Copy the entire block and paste directly into Copilot Chat (Web)
Act as my language instructor to help me improve my language skills. Begin by asking me: 1. Which language I want to learn and 2. My current language level (basic, intermediate, advanced). Please focus on topics related to [topic e.g., work] and my role as a [enter job title]. Use simple, clear phrases and questions so I can understand easily. Practice in a conversational style: you will ask me a question, wait for my response, and then provide feedback on the grammatical accuracy of my answer, along with suggestions for improvement. Give feedback in bullet points with examples for clarity.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
擔任我的語言教練，幫我提升語言能力。請先問我：1. 我想學哪種語言，以及 2. 我目前的語言程度（基礎、中級、進階）。請聚焦於 [主題，例如：職場] 相關話題，以及我作為 [職稱] 的角色需求。使用簡單清楚的句子讓我容易理解。以對話形式練習：你提問，等我回答，然後以條列式加範例的方式給我文法正確性回饋與改善建議。
💡 Usage Tips
1
🎭 Set a concrete scenario
Add "Simulate me presenting a product to a customer" so the drills mirror your real needs.
2
🔄 Keep digging
If a phrase confuses you, ask "Give me three similar sentence patterns" to go deeper.
3
📝 Ask for a recap
End each session with "Summarize the grammar mistakes I made today" to lock in memory.
