✨ Leadership Strengths Analysis
This helps you analyze your work and identify your 5 strengths and 3 areas of improvement, as well as how both impact your work. It also gives you 3 OKRs that you can use to improve. You can modify them as you see fit to ask for data or references.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
※ Copy the entire block and paste directly into Copilot Chat (Web)
Look at the emails, Teams meetings, documents, and Teams chats that I've sent in the last year. Use these messages to distill a leadership style document that I can use to inform Copilot of my personal voice and style. Give me a breakdown of my top 5 strengths in a table: | Strength | Description | How it helps me in my role | How it could be misunderstood | 2 ways I can build this strength. In another table, give me a breakdown of 3 areas for improvement: | Improvement area | Description | How it impacts my role | How it could be misunderstood | 3 ways I can improve. I would also like 3 Objectives and Key Results based on the above analysis that I could reach in the next 6-9 months.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
請檢閱我過去一年發送的郵件、Teams 會議、文件和 Teams 對話。利用這些訊息提煉一份領導風格文件，讓我可以用它告知 Copilot 我的個人聲音與風格。以表格呈現我的前五大優勢，格式：| 優勢 | 描述 | 如何幫助我發揮職務 | 可能被誤解的方式 | 兩種強化此優勢的方法。另以表格列出三個待改善領域，格式：| 改善領域 | 描述 | 對我職務的影響 | 可能被誤解的方式 | 三種改善方法。同時根據以上分析，提供三個我能在未來 6-9 個月達成的目標與關鍵結果（OKR）。
