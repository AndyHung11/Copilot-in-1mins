🕵️ AI Detection & Human Rewrite
✨ Detect the AI smell, then rewrite it back into human 🗣️
You spent ten minutes letting AI draft an internal announcement, a LinkedIn post, or some marketing copy, and it reads "smooth"—so smooth it has that unmistakable plastic feel: every sentence polished, stuffed with buzzwords, all "empowering" and "seamless integration," and it just doesn't sound like a person.

Worse, everyone can now spot AI-ghostwritten text at a glance—so shipping it can actually cost you.

This prompt splits the job into two steps: first it scores your text with a single AI likelihood (0–100%), telling you how machine-written it reads; then it hands you a clean, human-sounding rewrite—same meaning, minus the corporate filler, clichés, and AI tells—so it reads like someone actually sat down and wrote it.

Paste your text into Copilot Chat, run it, and seconds later you've got a score plus a rewrite. Blogs, internal comms, decks, LinkedIn, marketing drafts—anywhere authenticity matters.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
🕵️ AI Detection × Human Rewrite

Analyze the content below for AI-generated patterns.

Step 1 — AI Detection
Identify signs such as:
・Over-polished or perfectly structured writing
・Generic or corporate clichés and buzzwords
・Abstract language with low information density
・Unnatural tone or motivational phrasing
Output:
・One single AI Score (0–100%) indicating likelihood of AI-generated content

Step 2 — Clean Human Rewrite
Rewrite the content to sound more natural and human:
・Prefer clear, simple, direct wording
・Allow slight imperfections and natural flow
・Remove buzzwords, clichés, and corporate filler
・Preserve the original meaning and intent

Do NOT explain the changes. Output only:
1) AI Score
2) Clean rewritten version

[PASTE YOUR TEXT HERE]

🕵️ AI 味偵測 × 說人話改寫

分析以下內容是否有 AI 生成的特徵。

步驟一 — AI 偵測
找出以下訊號：
・過度工整、結構完美到不自然的寫法
・通用或公文腔的陳腔濫調、buzzword
・抽象、資訊密度很低的語句
・不自然或像在喊口號的激勵語氣
輸出：
・一個單一的 AI 指數（0–100%），代表這段文字是 AI 生成的可能性

步驟二 — 乾淨的人話改寫
把內容改寫得更自然、更像人：
・用清楚、簡單、直接的措辭
・容許一點點不完美與自然的語感
・拿掉 buzzword、陳腔濫調與公文填充
・保留原意與意圖

不要解釋你改了什麼。只輸出：
1）AI 指數
2）乾淨改寫後的版本
