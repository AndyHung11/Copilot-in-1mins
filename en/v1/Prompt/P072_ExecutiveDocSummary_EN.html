📄 Executive Doc Summary
✨ One page. Decision-ready. 🚀
The most important sentence in a 20-page report is almost never on page 1. By the time anyone reads to page 12, the meeting has started and the decision is already being guessed.

This prompt has Copilot collapse a long document into a one-page executive brief: the real point, the trade-offs, the recommended decision, and the risks—structured so a busy leader can act in 60 seconds.

No more "I'll get to it later." The decision happens now, with full context.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
Generate a concise, executive-ready summary for senior decision makers. Instructions: Identify the document [DOCUMENT] purpose and intended stakeholders. Extract the 3-5 most critical insights or outcomes. Clearly state decisions required or recommendations. Highlight risks, open questions, or constraints. Keep total length under 250 words. Structure: • Purpose: One sentence explaining why this document exists • Key Findings: Most important point / Second most important / Third most important • Decisions / Recommendations: Action or decision (include owner if mentioned) • Risks / Concerns: Explicitly stated risks or gaps • Next Steps: What should happen next Constraints: Use wording from the original document. Do not add opinions or assumptions. Flag if conclusions or decisions are missing. If too complex, recommend a section-by-section summary.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
請為資深決策者生成一份簡潔、即用的執行摘要。

指示：識別文件 [文件] 的目的與目標利害關係人；萃取 3-5 條最關鍵的洞察或結論；清楚說明所需決策或建議；標出風險、待解問題或限制條件；總字數限 250 字以內。

結構：
• 目的：一句話說明此文件為何存在
• 核心洞察：最重要的發現（第一點 / 第二點 / 第三點）
• 決策 / 建議：行動或決策（如有提及，附上負責人）
• 風險 / 疑慮：文件中明確提及的風險或缺口
• 後續步驟：接下來應發生的事

限制條件：使用原文措辭；不添加觀點或臆測；如缺少結論或決策，請標示；如過於複雜，建議逐節摘要。
