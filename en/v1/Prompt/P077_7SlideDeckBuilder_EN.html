🎯 7-Slide Deck Builder
✨ Brief in. Deck out. In minutes. 🚀
A client drops abrief at 4pm. You're pitching tomorrow. No templates, no add-ins, no designer—just you, Copilot, and that block of text.

This prompt has Copilot turn any messy brief into a structured 7-slide deck: title, problem/context, solution in 3 pillars, value & outcomes, delivery plan, evidence, and next steps—with 2–4 bullet speaker notes per slide.

Missing details? Copilot inserts [Info needed: …] placeholders so you know exactly what to chase. Perfect for proposals, status updates, and internal pitches.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
Create a concise 7-slide deckfrom the brief below. Inputs: Use only the pasted text as the source. Slides (exactly): Title — [PROJECT/PROPOSAL NAME] Problem/Context Proposed Solution (3 pillars max) Value & Outcomes (quantify if possible) Delivery Plan & Milestones (timeline in simple bullets) Evidence/Example (case, pilot, or proxy) Next Steps & Call to Action Requirements: Add concise speaker notes to each slide (2–4 bullets). Use clear, readable bullets (max 5 per slide). No branding or images; neutral theme is fine. If crucial details are missing, insert a placeholder bullet [Info needed: …]. Brief Starts: [PASTE BRIEF HERE] Brief Ends.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
根據下方的 Brief，產生一份精簡的 7 頁簡報。輸入：僅以貼上的文字作為來源。投影片（共 7 頁，順序固定）：1) 標題 — [專案／提案名稱] 2) 問題／脈絡 3) 解決方案（最多 3 個支柱）4) 價值與成果（盡量量化）5) 交付計畫與里程碑（簡單的時間軸 bullet）6) 案例／實證（Case、Pilot 或代理指標）7) 下一步與行動呼籲。要求：每頁加上 2–4 個 bullet 的演講備註；使用清楚、好讀的條列（每頁最多 5 條）；不需品牌或圖片，中性主題即可；若關鍵細節缺失，請插入占位符 [Info needed: …]。Brief 開始：[此處貼上 Brief] Brief 結束。
💡 Usage Tips
1. Paste the prompt directly into Copilot in PowerPoint for best results.
2. Replace [PROJECT/PROPOSAL NAME] with your real project name and paste the brief in full.
3. When you see [Info needed: …], add the missing detail and Copilot will fill it back in.
