THIS ISSUE · PROMPT
🪞 Spot Your Work Energy Patterns
✨ What energizes you, frustrates you, and what you keep avoiding—all in one look 🪞
Deep down you've had this feeling: some tasks light you up, some drain you the moment you touch them, and a few you keep pushing to next week—without ever quite knowing why.

You weren't imagining it. The signals have been there all along, just scattered across your weekly updates, to-dos, notes, and status messages, with no one to connect the dots.

This Prompt lets Copilot do what it does best: spot the signals and find the patterns. Paste in your recent updates, journal, or work notes and ask:

"Can you find a pattern in what energizes me, what frustrates me, or what I tend to avoid?"

The energy dips, the wins, the things you keep putting off—yup, it's all there. And the point is: once you can see it, you can finally do something about it. Use it on its own (just paste your updates), or pair it with a weekly reflection log and feed it a few weeks of data for an even clearer pattern.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
🪞 Work-energy self check

Can you find a pattern in the content I give you—what energizes me, what frustrates me, or what I tend to avoid?

Please answer in three parts:
1) What gives me energy
2) What frustrates or drains me
3) What I seem to keep avoiding
And for each part, suggest one small change I can try right away.

(Here are my recent updates / weekly notes / journal:)

💡 Usage Tips
1. 📝 Build up a few weeks of data — pair it with a weekly reflection log; the more you feed it, the clearer the pattern—don't just drop in a day or two.
2. 🎯 Give it context — paste weekly updates, to-dos, and meeting notes together; the more angles Copilot sees, the closer its read.
3. 🔁 Revisit monthly — rerun it on a schedule to track how your energy and avoidance lists shift—treat it as a personal growth dashboard.
