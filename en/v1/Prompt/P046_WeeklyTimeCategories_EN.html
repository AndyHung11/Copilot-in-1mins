💬 Weekly Time Categories
✨ See exactly where your week goes
You finished a packed week and somehow can't say what you actually spent time on. This prompt has Copilot scan your upcoming calendar and group your time into five categories, each with a short description.

You'll also get an estimated percentage for each bucket — instant clarity on where your week really goes. From blurry busy to crystal clear 📊
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
Looking at my calendar over this coming week, create five categories that describe how I spend my time. Give me a short description for each category and estimate the % time I spend on each.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
依據我接下來這一週的日曆，建立 五個 描述我如何運用時間的類別，並為每個類別提供簡短說明，以及估計我在每個類別上花費的時間百分比。
💡 Usage Tips
1. Paste the prompt directly into Copilot Chat.
2. Replace any bracketed placeholders with your own context.
3. Ask Copilot to refine or adjust the output as needed.
