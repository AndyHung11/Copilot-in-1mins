THIS ISSUE · PROMPT
📈 Benchmark Your Campaign Metrics
✨ Upload your numbers, benchmark them, cite sources, get a slide-ready table 📊
You wrap up a marketing or internal-comms campaign and your boss asks, "So, was it any good?"—you stare at open rates and click-throughs but have no idea if they're high or low, because you've got nothing to compare against.

This prompt has Copilot benchmark your campaign metrics against industry standards, flag which numbers are winning and which lag, and cite its sources so the figures hold up. Best of all, it lays everything into a PowerPoint-ready table with columns: Metric / My Campaign / Industry Benchmark / Commentary.

How to use it: Paste the prompt below into Copilot Chat (swap in your objective), run it, then upload your metrics file (Excel/CSV)—Copilot compares and builds the table.

Because "pretty good" hits different when you can actually prove it ✨.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
📈 Campaign benchmark (paste into Copilot Chat, then upload your metrics file)

How do these email campaign metrics measure up against industry benchmarks? This is an internal communications campaign with the objective of {your objective}. Cite your sources and provide the performance of this email campaign against benchmarks, and include these headers in the table: Metric | My Campaign | Industry Benchmark | Commentary (how did it compare summary). Export the results into a table that I can put into PowerPoint.

(Then upload your metrics file)
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
📈 Campaign 成效對標（貼進 Copilot Chat，執行後上傳你的數據檔）

這些 email campaign 的指標，跟業界標竿相比表現如何？這是一個內部溝通 campaign，目標是 {你的目標}。請引用你的資料來源，提供本次 email campaign 對比標竿的表現，並在表格中包含以下欄位：指標（Metric）｜ 我的數據（My Campaign）｜ 業界標竿（Industry Benchmark）｜ 評語（Commentary，如何比較的總結）。請把結果輸出成一張我可以貼進 PowerPoint 的表格。

（接著上傳你的 metrics 檔案）
💡 Usage Tips
1. 📤 Don't forget to upload — after pasting the prompt, upload your metrics file (Excel/CSV with open rate, click rate, etc.) so Copilot has something to compare. No license needed—just upload.
2. 🎯 Spell out objective and industry — replace "{your objective}" with the real goal (e.g., "drive event sign-ups") and name the industry so Copilot finds the right benchmarks.
3. 🔍 Ask for plain-English commentary — follow up with "in the Commentary column, say in one line whether each metric is good and suggest a next step" for insight, not just numbers.
