🛡️ Structured Risk Assessment
✨ Risk register × likelihood×impact scoring × heat map × top-3 priorities—in one pass 🛡️
A project's kicking off, a contract's about to be signed, an investment needs a decision—you know there are risks, but they're scattered across docs, emails, and meeting notes, hard to surface one by one and harder to rank.

List risks by gut feel and you'll miss the critical ones while blowing small ones out of proportion—leaving decision-makers unable to see what matters.

This prompt gives you a structured risk-assessment framework: it only surfaces risks explicitly stated or strongly implied, tags each with a category (Strategic / Operational / Financial / Compliance / Reputational / Technical), likelihood (1–5), and impact (1–5), computes Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact, and attaches specific, actionable mitigations.

You also get a risk heat map (High/Medium/Low counts), the top 3 risks needing immediate attention, and the information gaps that block a complete assessment—no catastrophizing, nothing invented, so decisions rest on evidence.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
🛡️ Structured risk assessment (paste into Copilot; best in the Researcher Agent)

You are conducting a structured risk assessment. Task: analyze the provided information for risks. Framework: for each risk provide | Risk ID | Description | Category | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Score | Mitigation |. Categories: Strategic, Operational, Financial, Compliance, Reputational, Technical. Likelihood scale: 1 = Rare (<5%), 2 = Unlikely (5–25%), 3 = Possible (25–50%), 4 = Likely (50–75%), 5 = Almost Certain (>75%). Impact scale: 1 = Negligible, 2 = Minor, 3 = Moderate, 4 = Major, 5 = Severe. Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact. Instructions: only identify risks explicitly mentioned or strongly implied; provide specific, actionable mitigations; do not catastrophize or invent unlikely scenarios; focus on the top 5–10 risks; note any assumptions. Output also includes: risk heat-map summary (High/Medium/Low counts), top 3 risks requiring immediate attention, and information gaps that prevent a complete assessment.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
🛡️ 結構化風險評估（貼進 Copilot；建議用 Researcher Agent）

你正在進行一次結構化風險評估。任務：分析所提供的資訊，找出其中的風險。框架：為每一條風險提供 | 風險 ID | 描述 | 類別 | 機率 | 影響 | 風險分數 | 緩解措施 |。類別：策略、營運、財務、合規、聲譽、技術。機率量表：1＝罕見（<5%）、2＝不太可能（5–25%）、3＝可能（25–50%）、4＝很可能（50–75%）、5＝幾乎確定（>75%）。影響量表：1＝可忽略、2＝輕微、3＝中等、4＝重大、5＝嚴重。風險分數＝機率 × 影響。指示：只辨識明確提到或強烈暗示的風險；提供具體可行的緩解措施；不誇大、不虛構不太可能的情境；聚焦最重要的 5～10 個風險；標註所做的假設。輸出另含：風險熱區圖摘要（高／中／低數量）、需立即處理的前三大風險、妨礙完整評估的資訊缺口。
💡 Usage Tips
1. 🔑 M365 Copilot license required — this prompt is designed for the Researcher Agent (advanced reasoning) and requires an M365 Copilot license; you can also paste it into Copilot Chat with your provided material.
2. 📎 Give it enough material first — paste or upload the documents, contracts, project briefs, or meeting notes you want assessed so risk identification is accurate; the more complete the input, the more reliable the likelihood and impact scores.
3. 🎯 Calibrate the scales and assumptions — check whether its likelihood/impact ratings make sense and adjust for your real context; pay attention to the assumptions and information gaps it flags, fill them, and re-run.
