🏆 Make Your Proposal Win
✨ Sharpen value, add differentiators, ROI & proof—seven moves to a more persuasive proposal 🏆
Your proposal is done and complete—but you know it: it's not persuasive enough. Why should the client pick you over a competitor? Is the ROI section clear? Will a decision-maker skip your executive summary in three seconds?

This prompt has Copilot, right in Word, polish your proposal into a "more likely to win" version across seven angles: strengthen the value proposition and client benefits, add differentiators vs. competitors, include risk-mitigation statements, improve the ROI and business case, add success stories and social proof, sharpen the executive summary for decision-makers, and refine the pricing presentation for clarity and perceived value.

The key part: it keeps your original structure—it just makes every section more persuasive. Not a rewrite from scratch, an upgrade of the same proposal.

How to use it: open your proposal in Word, bring up Copilot, and paste the prompt below to optimize it in one pass. Walk into your next pitch with better odds 🏆.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
🏆 Optimize a proposal (bring up Copilot inside Word and paste)

Review this proposal and enhance it for winning: 1) Strengthen value proposition (client benefits), 2) Add compelling differentiators vs. competition, 3) Include risk mitigation statements, 4) Improve ROI and business case section, 5) Add social proof or success stories, 6) Enhance executive summary for decision-makers, 7) Refine pricing presentation for clarity and value perception. Maintain original structure but make it more persuasive.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
🏆 提案優化（在 Word 內叫出 Copilot 貼上）

請檢視這份提案並優化它以提高勝率：1) 強化價值主張（客戶效益），2) 加入對比競爭對手的有力差異化，3) 納入風險緩解說明，4) 改善 ROI 與商業論證章節，5) 加入成功案例或社會證明，6) 為決策者優化 Executive Summary，7) 調整報價呈現方式以提升清晰度與價值感。請保留原本的結構，但讓內容更有說服力。
💡 Usage Tips
1. 📄 Open your proposal in Word first — this prompt edits your open proposal with Copilot inside Word, which requires an M365 Copilot license.
2. 🎯 Give it context and rivals — tell Copilot who the client is, your main competitors, and what you most want to win on, so the differentiators and value proposition land sharper.
3. 🔍 Review each section before accepting — after the seven-angle pass, check each section; especially don't let it invent ROI numbers or success stories—swap in your real data.
