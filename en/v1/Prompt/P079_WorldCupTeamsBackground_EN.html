⚽ World Cup Teams Background
✨ From blurry bookshelf to fever pitch — pick a country, hit play 🚀
The World Cup kicks off June 11, and your Teams video background is still that blurry bookshelf or the same old office wall. You'd love a background repping your team, but you don't do Photoshop and you're scared of ugly color combos—so it never happens.

This prompt runs straight in Copilot image generation: just swap [country] for the team you're rooting for (e.g. [Japan], [Brazil], [USA]), and Copilot auto-infers that country's flag colors, paints them as abstract hand-brushed strokes, adds stylized soccer balls and a World Cup trophy silhouette, and centers the country name as the only text—16:9, optimized for Teams backgrounds.

No design skills, no stock hunting. 30 seconds to a background that stands out more than anyone else's on the call.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
Ole Ole Ole Ole. Let's Play! ⚽

Generate a Microsoft Teams background image with these specs:

🎨 Style | Painted, illustrative (not photorealistic), using abstract hand-painted brush strokes and layered paint textures.
🌈 Palette | Auto-infer the color palette from the official flag colors of [USA] — don't make me specify them. No actual flags or country symbols.
🔤 Text | Include the country name — [USA] — as the only text: clean, centered, naturally integrated. No other words or text.
⚽ Elements | Add stylized, painted (non-realistic) soccer balls, and a World Cup trophy with an accurate silhouette and proportions, in a simplified painted style (no branding, no extra markings, no text on the trophy).
🚫 Avoid | No people, crowds, faces, mascots, or readable symbols beyond the country name.
📐 Spec | Aspect ratio 16:9, optimized for Microsoft Teams backgrounds, key visuals centered and crop-safe. Communicate "World Cup celebration" through color, motion, and illustration.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
Ole Ole Ole Ole. Let's Play! ⚽

請生成一張 Microsoft Teams 視訊背景圖，需求如下：

🎨 風格｜手繪插畫風格（非寫實照片），以抽象筆刷與層疊塗料質感呈現。
🌈 配色｜依 [國家] 的官方國旗顏色自動推斷配色，不需我另外指定；背景不要出現任何實際國旗或國家象徵符號。
🔤 文字｜將國家名稱「[國家]」作為圖中唯一文字，乾淨、置中、自然融入設計，不要有其他任何文字。
⚽ 元素｜加入風格化的手繪足球，以及一座世界盃獎盃（剪影與比例正確，但簡化為手繪非寫實風格；無品牌、無多餘標記、獎盃上無文字）。
🚫 避免｜不要出現人物、人群、臉孔、吉祥物，或國家名稱以外可辨識的符號。
📐 規格｜比例 16:9，專為 Microsoft Teams 背景優化，主視覺置中並避開介面裁切區；整體以色彩、動態與插畫傳達「世界盃慶典」氛圍。
💡 Usage Tips
1. Paste the full prompt into Copilot image generation (M365 Copilot Chat / Copilot web), replacing both [country] placeholders with your team's English name (e.g. [Japan], [Brazil], [Argentina]).
2. Once the image is generated, send a follow-up: "Mirror this image left-to-right, including the text," so Copilot produces the mirrored version.
3. Save the mirrored image, then in a Teams meeting → Video effects → Add new → upload the mirrored image (so the text reads correctly to you on the call).
