📡 Predict Emerging Risks from Email Patterns
✨ Early warning signals × likelihood score × impact × owners and timeline for prevention 📡
Problems rarely erupt out of nowhere—they're usually hiding in the patterns of your email: a client's tone getting more urgent, a project's back-and-forth intensifying, an issue raised again and again but never resolved. By the time it catches fire, it's often too late.

This prompt has Copilot read your last 30 days of email patterns and predict which topics are likely to become future problems. For each potential issue it gives you seven things: an issue description, the warning indicators observed, a likelihood score (1–10), the potential impact if not addressed, recommended mitigation strategies, who should be involved in prevention, and a timeline for preventive action.

It then ranks everything by risk score (likelihood × impact) so you see what to tackle first. Instead of firefighting after a problem erupts, spot the early warning signals, align stakeholders proactively, and stop risks before they escalate.

💡 Usage Tips
1. 🔓 Turn on WorkIQ first — this prompt reads your email and other organizational data, so enable WorkIQ (formerly Work mode) in Copilot Chat and make sure you have an M365 Copilot license, or you'll only get generic output.
2. 🎯 Narrow the scope for accuracy — specify a client, project, or team (e.g. "only email with client X"), or change 30 days to the window you care about, so the prediction is sharper.
3. 🔍 Verify before acting — the warning indicators and likelihood are inferences from email patterns; check the original emails before acting and don't treat the AI's scores as final.
