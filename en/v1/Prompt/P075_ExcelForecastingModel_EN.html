💬 Excel Forecasting Model
✨ Build a real forecast in Excel 🚀
You have months of historical data sitting in Excel—and a stakeholder asking "so what's it going to look like next quarter?" Eyeballing a trendline isn't an answer.

This prompt has Copilot build a real forecasting model from your historical data: trend and seasonality analysis, a 3/6/12-month projection with confidence intervals, the assumptions behind it, and a sensitivity scenario for best/worst/likely cases.

You walk into the next review with numbers you can actually defend.
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
※ Copy the entire block and paste directly into Copilot Chat (Web)
Build a forecasting model for [METRIC] based on this historical data. Include: 1) Trend analysis and seasonality detection, 2) Forecast for next [3/6/12] months with confidence intervals, 3) Key assumptions underlying the forecast, 4) Factors that could change the forecast, 5) Sensitivity analysis (best case, worst case, most likely), 6) Recommended model formula and methodology, 7) Visualization of historical data vs. forecast. Explain model in business terms.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
請依據這份歷史資料，為 [指標名稱] 建立預測模型。請包含：
1) 趨勢分析與季節性偵測
2) 未來 [3／6／12] 個月的預測（含信心區間）
3) 預測底下的關鍵假設
4) 可能改變預測的因素
5) 敏感度分析（最好／最差／最可能）
6) 推薦的模型公式與方法論
7) 歷史 vs. 預測對照視覺化
最後請用商業語言解釋這個模型。
