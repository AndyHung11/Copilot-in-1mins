✉️ Meeting Follow Up Email
✨ Copy, send, done in a minute
Sorting out the follow-up email after a meeting is one of the most painful chores. Based on your meeting transcript, this prompt auto-generates a complete follow-up email: a decisions table, an action items + owners table, and a list of items to discuss next time. Copy, send, done in 1 minute ✉️
  📋 Prompt Template (English)
Write a follow-up email to the attendees of this meeting based on the [Meeting Transcript]. The email should include: A table showing the decisions made during the meeting. A table listing the actions to be taken, along with the owners responsible for each action. A list of considerations for the next meeting.
  📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
請根據 [會議逐字稿] 為與會者撰寫一封跟進信。信件應包含：一張呈現會議決議事項的表格；一張列出行動項目及各項目負責人的表格；以及下次會議的注意事項清單。
💡 Usage Tips
1. Paste the prompt directly into Copilot Chat.
2. Replace any bracketed placeholders with your own context.
3. Ask Copilot to refine or adjust the output as needed.
