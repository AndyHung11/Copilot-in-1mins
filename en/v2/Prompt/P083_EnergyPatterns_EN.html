🪞 Work-energy self check

Can you find a pattern in the content I give you—what energizes me, what frustrates me, or what I tend to avoid?

Please answer in three parts:
1) What gives me energy
2) What frustrates or drains me
3) What I seem to keep avoiding
And for each part, suggest one small change I can try right away.

💡 Three Ways to Use It
1
📝 Build up a few weeks of data
Pair it with a weekly reflection log; the more you feed it, the clearer the pattern—don't expect a verdict from one or two days.
2
🎯 Give it context
Paste weekly updates, to-dos, and meeting notes together—the more angles Copilot sees, the closer its read of the real you.
3
🔁 Revisit monthly
Rerun it on a schedule to track how your energy and avoidance lists shift—treat it as a personal growth dashboard.
