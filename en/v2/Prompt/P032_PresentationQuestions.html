Generate three engaging questions for each section of this presentation, along with suggested answers. These questions will be used as a basis for audience interaction during the presentation. Ensure the questions are relevant to the content of each section, make the audience reflect on their understanding, and encourage active participation.

為這份簡報的每個章節產出三個吸引人的問題，並附上建議答案。這些問題將作為簡報過程中與觀眾互動的基礎。請確保問題與各章節內容相關、能讓觀眾反思自身理解，並鼓勵積極參與。
