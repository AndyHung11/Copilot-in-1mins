🚀 Copy This Prompt (English Version)
Open Copilot Chat (Web or Work) and paste the prompt below.
Please review and optimize my presentation for an upcoming [type of meeting, e.g., customer briefing or internal workshop]. The meeting is scheduled for [duration, e.g. one hour], and I need to reserve time for [specific purpose, e.g. Q&A]. The presentation file is [insert file name or upload the file]. Analyze the file to identify any duplicate or redundant content across slides, and suggest ways to streamline the deck to save time without losing key messages. Ensure the tone and content are appropriate for a [audience type, e.g. customer-facing or executive-level] audience. Provide a structured report that includes a summary of findings, a list of duplicate or redundant content, an optimization proposal with recommendations for slide reduction and timing, and an estimate of the time saved. The report should be clear, actionable, and formatted for easy review.
📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
請針對即將到來的 [會議類型，例如：客戶簡報或內部工作坊] 檢閱並優化我的簡報。會議預計 [時長，例如：一小時]，我需要保留時間給 [特定用途，例如：問答環節]。簡報檔案為 [插入檔案名稱或上傳檔案]。請分析檔案，找出投影片間的重複或冗餘內容，並建議精簡方式以節省時間而不失去關鍵訊息。確保語氣和內容適合 [受眾類型，例如：面向客戶或高階主管] 受眾。提供結構化報告，包含：發現摘要、重複或冗餘內容清單、含投影片縮減與時間規劃建議的優化方案，以及預估節省的時間。報告應清晰、可行，且易於審閱。
