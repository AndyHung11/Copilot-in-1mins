🚀 Copy This Prompt (English Version)
📝 Update a Word doc with tracked changes (bring up Copilot inside Word and paste)

Update the open Word document using the relevant information from [meetings, emails, files, notes]. Preserve the document's existing formatting and structure, and track all edits as redlined changes so I can review them before accepting.
📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
📝 用追蹤修訂更新 Word 文件（在 Word 內叫出 Copilot 貼上）

請使用來自 〔會議、電子郵件、檔案、筆記〕 的相關資訊，更新目前開啟的 Word 文件。保留文件既有的格式與結構，並將所有修改以追蹤修訂（紅線）方式標記，讓我可以逐一檢視後再決定接受。
💡 Three Ways to Use It
1
🔓 Turn on WorkIQ first
To pull from your meetings, emails, and files, enable WorkIQ (formerly Work mode) in Copilot, or it can't reach your org content.
2
📂 Name specific sources
Replace the brackets with concrete sources (e.g. "last Thursday's project meeting notes," "the quote email to the client") so Copilot knows where to look and fills accurately.
3
👀 Review one by one
After it runs, open Word's Review tab to walk through each tracked change, then Accept All once verified and reject any you're unsure about.
