📈 Campaign benchmark (paste into Copilot Chat, then upload your metrics file)

How do these email campaign metrics measure up against industry benchmarks? This is an internal communications campaign with the objective of {your objective}. Cite your sources and provide the performance of this email campaign against benchmarks, and include these headers in the table: Metric | My Campaign | Industry Benchmark | Commentary (how did it compare summary). Export the results into a table that I can put into PowerPoint.

(Then upload your metrics file)
📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
📈 Campaign 成效對標（貼進 Copilot Chat，執行後上傳你的數據檔）

這些 email campaign 的指標，跟業界標竿相比表現如何？這是一個內部溝通 campaign，目標是 {你的目標}。請引用你的資料來源，提供本次 email campaign 對比標竿的表現，並在表格中包含以下欄位：指標（Metric）｜ 我的數據（My Campaign）｜ 業界標竿（Industry Benchmark）｜ 評語（Commentary，如何比較的總結）。請把結果輸出成一張我可以貼進 PowerPoint 的表格。

（接著上傳你的 metrics 檔案）
💡 Three Ways to Use It
1
📤 Don't forget to upload
After pasting the prompt, upload your metrics file (Excel/CSV with open rate, click rate, etc.) so Copilot has something to compare. No license needed—just upload.
2
🎯 Spell out objective and industry
Replace "{your objective}" with the real goal (e.g., "drive event sign-ups") and name the industry so Copilot finds the right benchmarks.
3
🔍 Ask for plain-English commentary
Follow up with "in the Commentary column, say in one line whether each metric is good and suggest a next step" for insight, not just numbers.
Next time they ask "was it good?"—prove it with data ▸▸
