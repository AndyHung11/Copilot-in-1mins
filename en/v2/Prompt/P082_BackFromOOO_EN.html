🏝️ The back-from-OOO trio (type these after tapping Copilot in Outlook)

1. Summarize the week
Summarize emails I missed last week

2. Catch up on a project
Catch me up on Project Sigma updates from the past 5 days

3. Pull out the to-dos
Highlight action items from unread messages
📋 提示詞範本（繁體中文版）
🏝️ 休假回歸三連發（在 Outlook 點開 Copilot 後輸入）

1. 整週摘要
Summarize emails I missed last week
（幫我摘要上週我錯過的信件）

2. 追特定專案
Catch me up on Project Sigma updates from the past 5 days
（幫我追過去 5 天 Project Sigma 的最新進度）

3. 抽出待辦
Highlight action items from unread messages
（從未讀訊息裡標出需要我處理的行動項目）
💡 Three Ways to Use It
1
📅 Pin the time range
Say "the past 5 days," "last week," or "the 10 days I was out"—the tighter the range, the sharper the summary, and no old mail sneaks in.
2
🧵 Name a project or person
"Catch me up on Project X" or "summarize threads from [boss]" follows just that line instead of dumping everything at once.
3
✅ Ask for action items
After catching up, add "Highlight action items from unread" or "what needs my reply today" to turn the summary into a to-do list.
