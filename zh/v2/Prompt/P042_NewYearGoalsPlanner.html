📋 Prompt Template (English)
It's the start of 2025, and I want to make the most of this year in my professional life. Help me outline strategies for setting and achieving impactful business goals. I'd also like suggestions for fostering collaboration, improving productivity, and maintaining a healthy work-life balance throughout the year.
💡 三個情境式玩法
1. 🎯 個人年度 OKR 設定
把模糊的新年願望轉化為可追蹤的 OKR 框架，每季回顧時有明確依據。
2. 🤝 團隊目標對齊
將個人目標連結到團隊方向，確保你的成長也為組織創造價值。
3. ⚖️ 工作生活平衡規劃
預先規劃充電假期與減壓策略，在年中倦怠發生前就先做好預防。
